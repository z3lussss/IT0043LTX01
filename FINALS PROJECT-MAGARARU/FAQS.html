How do your gaming experiences influence your approach to game development?

My experiences as a gamer significantly influence my approach to game development. Playing a wide variety of games helps me understand what makes a game enjoyable and engaging from a player's perspective.

What do you think is the most important skill for a game developer?

the most important skill for a game developer is a strong foundation in programming coupled with creativity. Technical skills are crucial because they enable you to implement complex game mechanics and solve technical problems. However, creativity is equally important as it drives innovation in game design, helping to create unique and compelling gameplay experiences that stand out in a crowded market.

Why did you choose IT with a focus on game development?

I chose IT with a focus on game development because I've always been passionate about video games, both playing and understanding how they are made. This specialization allows me to combine my technical skills and creativity to craft engaging gaming experiences.
